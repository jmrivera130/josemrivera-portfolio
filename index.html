About Me

Hello! I'm Jose. My journey into UX design began with a deep curiosity for understanding people and a desire to make technology more accessible and enjoyable for everyone. I believe that the best designs emerge from a blend of empathy, research, and iterative problem-solving.

I thrive on untangling complex problems and translating user needs into clean, elegant, and effective solutions. While I'm at the beginning of my career, I'm a fast learner, a dedicated collaborator, and I'm eager to contribute to a team that values thoughtful design and user advocacy.

My Foundational Design Process

I follow a flexible but structured design process to ensure every decision is informed by user needs and business goals.

1. Discover & Empathize

I start by diving deep into the problem space through user interviews, surveys, and competitive analysis to build a foundation of empathy.

2. Define & Ideate

Synthesizing research into key insights, I define the core user needs. Then, I brainstorm and sketch a wide range of potential solutions.

3. Design & Prototype

I create wireframes, high-fidelity mockups, and interactive prototypes to bring the best ideas to life, focusing on usability and aesthetics.

4. Test & Iterate

I validate designs through usability testing, gathering feedback to refine and improve the solution in a continuous cycle of iteration.

My AI-Accelerated Workflow

I also leverage cutting-edge AI tools to enhance efficiency and creativity, delivering high-quality results at an accelerated pace.

1. Accelerated Discovery & Synthesis

I use AI to rapidly analyze research data, identify patterns, and generate user personas and journey maps. This combines discovery and definition into one fluid, data-driven phase.

2. Rapid Prototyping & Design

Leveraging generative AI for ideation and wireframing, I can explore a wider range of design concepts quickly. This allows for faster creation of high-fidelity, interactive prototypes.

3. Continuous AI-Powered Iteration

AI tools help in analyzing usability test results and predicting user behavior, enabling me to test hypotheses and iterate on designs with greater speed and accuracy.
